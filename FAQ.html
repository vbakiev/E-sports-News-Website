Q: Do I need Xbox Live Gold to play Fortnite Battle Royale?
A: Yes. You do need Xbox Live Gold in order to play Fortnite Battle Royale on Xbox.

Q: Do I need PlayStation Plus to play Fortnite Battle Royale?
A: No. You do not need PS Plus in order to play Fortnite Battle Royale on PlayStation.

Q: Do I need Nintendo Switch Online to play Fortnite Battle Royale?
A: No. You do not need Nintendo Switch Online in order to play Fortnite Battle Royale on Switch.

Q: How do you handle teamers in solo play?
A: We’ve built a system to automatically ban teamers in solo play.

Q: How are you handling cheaters (aimbots, overlays, etc)?
A: We take cheating very seriously on both a legal and game development front. We don’t want to tip our hand by talking in-depth about how we combat cheating.

Q: I would like to report a player, where can I do so?
A:You can use the in-game feedback tool located in the Main Menu. There is also an option to report a player in-game when spectating them.

Q: Will you add a first-person option?
A: No. We’re not planning to add a first-person option. We don’t feel it fits with the design style of Fortnite. We may revisit in the future.

Q: Will you add more maps?
A: Yes, but not right away. Currently, we’ll continue making improvements to our existing map, including adding more points of interest.

Q: Will Fortnite Battle Royale be pay-to-win?
A: No. We will not sell items that give a competitive advantage.

Q: How do I change my preferred language?
A: Once in-game, navigate to the drop-down Options menu on the top right of the client.

1.Click the Settings gear icon.

2. Select the Game tab at the top of the screen.

3.Confirm your preferred Language in the Region section.

4.Once you’ve finished, be sure to Apply your changes.
